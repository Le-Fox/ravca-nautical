Who We Are

Reliability. Efficiency. Responsiveness.

With over 15 years of dedicated service to the maritime industry, Ravca Nautical provides dependable sourcing, supply, and consulting solutions tailored to the needs of vessels, shipping companies, and maritime professionals.

Backed by more than 25 years of seafaring and industry experience, we understand the critical importance of speed and reliability in maritime logistics. From general ship requisites and medical supplies to specialised maritime consulting and international networking services, our team is committed to delivering practical solutions that keep your operations running smoothly.
